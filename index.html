About me

Picture this: It’s 2020, and like everyone else, I’m in my pajamas, on my third cup of coffee, juggling a Zoom meeting with my left hand while trying to convince my cat that my keyboard is not a pillow. The world went remote, and I discovered that, actually, I thrive in this chaos.

The pandemic introduced me to the joys (and quirks) of working from home, and I quickly realized virtual assistance is my sweet spot. I get to help entrepreneurs, small business owners, and busy professionals get their work done—minus the cat interruptions (usually). Whether it’s managing calendars, tackling email overload, or setting up that perfect Zoom call (yes, I will remind you to unmute), I’m here to make life easier.

With a blend of tech-savviness and a dash of humor, I navigate digital tools like a pro—streamlining workflows, boosting productivity, and keeping things running smoothly. From client communications to task management, I’m your go-to person for getting things done, all while enjoying the remote life that fits me perfectly.
